Child Safety Standards

Meant to Be  ·  Last updated: April 19, 2026

Meant to Be is a dating app strictly for adults aged 18 and over. We are committed to maintaining a safe platform and have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) in any form.

1 Age Requirement & Enforcement

  • All users must confirm they are 18 or older during sign-up.
  • Every account undergoes mandatory identity verification: users submit a selfie that is reviewed by our moderation team, and an AI estimate of the user's age is checked against their stated date of birth.
  • Accounts where the verified age is below 18 are immediately removed.
  • If we have reason to believe a user is under 18, their account is suspended and removed without notice.

2 Prohibited Content & Conduct

The following are strictly prohibited on Meant to Be:

  • Any content that sexually exploits or endangers minors (CSAM / CSAE)
  • Grooming, solicitation, or any communication targeting minors
  • Sharing, distributing, or requesting sexually explicit material involving anyone under 18
Violation of these rules results in immediate account termination and, where required by law, reporting to the relevant authorities including the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC).

3 Reporting & Moderation

  • Users can report any other user directly from within the app at any time.
  • All reports are reviewed by our moderation team.
  • Accounts found to violate our child safety standards are permanently banned.
  • We report any discovered CSAM to NCMEC via the CyberTipline as required by US law (18 U.S.C. § 2258A), and cooperate with law enforcement requests.
